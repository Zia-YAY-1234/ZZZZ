About Us

Canyon Park students enjoy a tradition of excellence in our academic and extracurricular programs that encourage life-long learning. From our Award Winning Science Olympiad Teams and History Day groups to our outstanding Music programs, Canyon Park meets the needs of a diverse student population.
The four pillars of Canyon Park; Community, Respect, Responsibility, and Excellence are observable in every aspect of the school.
Our staff is thoroughly committed to our students' academic successes as well as their development as happy, healthy, productive, responsible citizens of an ever changing world.
We maintain several programs to help guide our students through their middle school years.Collaboration within the Staff ensures that every student receives the best quality instruction with an emphasis on the whole student. Course offerings include: AAP, challenge classes, art, orchestra, band, choir, drama, multimedia technology, Video production, Health/Fitness and Career and Technical Education (CTE) classes.Extracurricular activities and sports provide enrichment with a large variety of choices. In addition, Canyon Park students pride themselves in supporting our school and giving to our community. We have an active PTSA, a WEB (Where Everyone Belongs) program for our new students, a weekly Video Bulletin featuring our students, and an active ASB leadership group.
